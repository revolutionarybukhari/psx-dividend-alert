# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and don't bring stock-tip arguments, religious arguments, or political arguments into issues and PRs. We're here to make a small tool work well.

## Reporting

If someone behaves in a way that makes you not want to keep contributing, open a private issue or email the maintainer listed in `package.json`. Reports stay confidential.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may delete comments, close issues, or restrict accounts that violate this policy. Repeated violations result in a permanent ban from the repo.
